Hyesung Jeon

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jae-Joon Kim. I received my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in Feb. 2023.

My research focuses on efficient deep learning, including model compression for generative vision and language models, quantization-aware training, kv cache compression, and hardware-software co-design for AI acceleration. I am broadly interested in designing inference and training systems that are fast, memory-efficient, and hardware-friendly.

Research

Keywords: Efficient Deep Learning, Model Compression, Hardware-Software Co-Design

My primary research focus is the design of efficient inference and training systems for AI models. I have been working on model compression for generative vision and language models, and I plan to expand my research into advanced AI agents and their inference acceleration techniques, including compiler design and system-level acceleration architectures.

In particular, my research interests lie in:

  • Deep learning system frameworks and algorithms for efficient inference and training
  • Model compression techniques, including quantization and pruning
  • Hardware-friendly deep learning model architecture design
  • Deep neural network accelerator system design
